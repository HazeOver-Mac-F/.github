## 🖌 Overview  
HazeOver Advanced is a focus enhancement application that automatically dims all background windows when you switch to an active application, reducing the visual noise of a busy desktop and helping your attention stay where the work is happening.

The dimming intensity is adjustable from a subtle tint to a near-complete blackout, letting you calibrate the effect to your preference and the ambient lighting conditions of your environment.

Transitions between applications are smooth and animated, with a gentle fade that guides your eye to the newly active window rather than creating a harsh visual jump between states.

Multiple display support applies the dimming effect correctly across all connected monitors, keeping inactive windows dark on every screen while the active window remains fully lit on its own display.

For anyone who works with many windows open simultaneously and struggles to maintain focus on the current task, HazeOver Advanced provides a simple, elegant visual intervention that makes concentration noticeably easier.
